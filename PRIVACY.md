# Privacy Policy — Sheets Range to Image

_Last updated: 2026_

**Sheets Range to Image does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
data or user content.**

## What the extension does

- It runs only on `https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/*`.
- When you choose **Convert Selected Range to Image**, it asks Google Sheets to
  copy your current selection and reads the resulting styled‑HTML from the
  **clipboard** in order to render an image of those cells.
- The image is generated entirely **locally in your browser**. It is shown in an
  in‑page dialog and is only saved or copied when you click **Download PNG** or
  **Copy to Clipboard**.

## Data handling

- **No data leaves your device.** The extension makes no network requests and has
  no servers, analytics, or third‑party services.
- **No storage.** The extension does not retain your cell data, images, or any
  other information after the dialog is closed.
- **Clipboard.** Generating the image uses the clipboard to obtain the selection,
  so your clipboard will contain the copied cells afterward. The clipboard is
  used only on your device and is never read except to build the requested image.

## Permissions

- `clipboardRead` / `clipboardWrite` — used solely to read the copied selection
  and to place a generated image on your clipboard when you ask.
